Meet the Designer

Sam has been interested in design since before he realized. After discovering his true calling freshman year of college, he quickly switched his major and fell in love. Spending most of his time with computer graphics and print technology, Sam found it was time to add more skills to his toolbox, and searched out web development after graduating with his Bachelor of Art from the University of Evansville in the spring of 2014. Thinkful caught his attention quickly into the search, and he fell even more in love with HTML5, CSS3, and jQuery. Although he plans to climb ladders in the design world, his skills in web development and design will add an edge that can’t be beat.

The big city calls, and with it, interests in photography, animals, Apple products, ceramics, modern & contemporary art, print production, food, and the rush of travelling to places unknown. Sam loves troubleshooting problems, and has an even greater love for being able to create what he wants without boundries or assignments.

Don’t let the small town atmosphere Sam grew up in fool you, this young artist is ready to stretch his wings and create art and code all over the world.
